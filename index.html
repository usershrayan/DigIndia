A Corruption-Free India, Digitally Empowered

DigIndia envisions a future where citizens have direct control and oversight over governance, eliminating corruption and inefficiency through transparency, accountability, and technology-driven systems.

Our 6 Pillars for a New Governance Model

1. Administrative Reforms

  • Digitize all government departments with complete paperless processes stored securely on cloud servers.
  • Automate file clearances: if no objection rises within 30 days, files auto-clear to prevent bureaucratic delays.
  • Implement OTP-based complaint closures to avoid fraudulent resolution.
  • Introduce a citizen rating system (via Aadhaar OTP) for departments; ratings below 70% lead to senior officials' termination.
  • Enforce strict, automated penalties for officials who delay or neglect work, such as salary freezes and suspensions.

2. Judicial Reforms

  • Select judges through an independent, transparent panel free from political influence.
  • Use AI to assign court dates, preventing bribery and delays.
  • Establish fast-track anti-corruption courts in every district, with video-recorded proceedings accessible to involved parties.
  • Publicize annual judge performance reviews to ensure accountability and quality of justice.
  • Create an independent oversight body for removal or disciplinary action of errant judges.

3. Education Reforms

  • Form Parent Management Boards at private schools with rotating membership to regulate fees, teacher salaries, and expenses.
  • Enforce non-profit operation transparency for schools to prevent fee exploitation.
  • Ensure teacher accountability through fair pay and competency.
  • Implement UPSC-style anti-leak examination systems nationwide.
  • Introduce legal and civic education from early schooling to build awareness of rights and responsibilities.

4. Healthcare Reforms

  • Standardize pricing for surgeries, treatments, and consultations with government-notified rates.
  • Conduct real-time digital audits of hospital bills and services.
  • Impose strict penalties, including license cancellation and legal action against fraudulent practices.

5. Political & Electoral Reforms

  • Mandate 100% transparency in political funding and expenditures accessible online by citizens.
  • Make Election Commission an independent body with no political interference.
  • Bar candidates with corruption charges from contesting elections until fully acquitted.
  • Restrict political dynasties by allowing only one family member per generation to contest elections.
  • Introduce an Aadhaar-based biannual MP rating system; those scoring below 50% face a lifetime election ban.
  • Require parties to pre-select four candidates per seat, with citizens voting online for the final candidate.

6. Media & Social Reforms

  • Ban corporate and political ownership of media to ensure unbiased reporting.
  • Launch public integrity campaigns rewarding whistle-blowers and honesty advocates.
  • Promote societal shift from tolerance of corruption to zero acceptance through education and public recognition awards.
